# FD Note

Acknowledge only.

## Behavior

- Reply with one short acknowledgement.
- Do not repeat or summarize the user's note.
- Do not analyze, validate, or expand on it.
- Do not create a plan or TODO.
- Do not inspect files or run commands.
- Do not ask follow-up questions unless the note is actually unclear and requires a response.

## Acceptable Replies

Vary naturally. Examples:

- `Noted.`
- `Got it.`
- `Acknowledged.`
- `Understood.`
- `Logged for context.`
- `Makes sense.`

## Boundary

If the user asks for action, judgment, documentation, implementation, or durable storage, use a more appropriate skill instead.
